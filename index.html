Trading Rules
Non-negotiable — every single trade, every single day
#
Rule
Detail
1
4H candle rejects S/R — closed confirmation
Wait for the 4H candle to fully close above resistance or below support. A wick alone is not enough — the body must close beyond the level. This confirms institutional rejection, not just a temporary spike.
2
Draw Fibonacci before entering
Fibonacci must be drawn from the most recent swing high to swing low (or vice versa) on 4H or 1H BEFORE considering any entry. Target the 61.8%, 70.5%, or 78.6% retracement zones for confluence. No Fib drawn = no trade.
3
Draw a trendline for structural context
Mark the dominant trendline on 4H or Daily before entry. Price must respect the trendline or break it decisively with volume. A trendline break after an FVG fill or liquidity sweep is a premium entry signal.
4
Inside candle or reversal pattern on 1H or lower
After the 4H rejection, wait for a 1H, 15M, or 5M inside candle (candle that fits entirely within the prior candle) OR a reversal pattern — engulfing, pin bar, doji, hammer, or morning/evening star. This is your precise entry trigger.
5
Confirm BOS on 1M or above
A Break of Structure (BOS) must be confirmed on the 1M timeframe or higher in the direction of your trade. Price must break and close beyond the last significant swing high (for longs) or swing low (for shorts). CHoCH is also accepted as confirmation of a trend shift.
6
Define risk before entry
Know your stop loss in dollars BEFORE you click. No defined stop = no trade. Calculate position size using the calculator. Max 2% risk per trade on this challenge.
7
Minimum 1:3 risk-to-reward
Only take setups targeting at least 3× your risk. At 300× growth target, you need asymmetric wins. A 1:3 setup with 50% win rate grows the account. Anything less is gambling.
8
Max 2 trades per day
3+ trades = chasing. Quality only. Boredom trades blow accounts. After 2 trades, close your platform regardless of outcome.
9
Daily loss limit −15%
Hit −15% on the day? Close everything. Log off immediately. No exceptions. No revenge. Come back tomorrow with a clear mind.
10
Take partial profit at 1R
Close 40% of position at 1R. Move stop to breakeven immediately. Let 60% run to 2R–3R. This protects profit and lets winners breathe.
11
No trading during major news
CPI, FOMC, NFP, FOMC minutes — stay flat. Check ForexFactory before every session. Wait minimum 15 minutes after release before re-evaluating structure.
12
Never move stop against your trade
Moving a stop further away is the #1 account killer across all retail traders. Your stop was logical when you placed it. Take the loss. Protect what remains.
